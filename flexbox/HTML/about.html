About Us

A few words about us

Peaks-Pro Paints took its feet in the early 1970’s as a trading concern with the name “Peaks Pro Nigeria” in line with his quest, passion and drive to fashion a viable business, the Chairman/CEO, single-handedly supervised and nurtured the outfit basically with the concern of dealing on paints. By 1981, Peaks Pros Nigeria won the heart of discerning end-users of paints as a major distributor to so many manufacturing companies in Nigeria and abroad.

Consequent upon its position in the surface coating, the need to establish a paint company was muted. However, in 1986, production of Peaks Pro was started by the Chairman/CEO under the business name Saclux Industries Nigeria Limited. In the same 1986, SACLUX INDUSTRIES NIGERIA LIMITED was incorporated as a Limited Liability Company with RC. NO 79691. Today, the company has moved from Grace to Grace challenges notwithstanding giving birth to other companies viz.

1. ROSHED INDUSTRIES NIGERIA LIMITED - MAKERS OF SAPIL PLASTICS PRODUCTS & POLYTHENE SHOPPING BAGS AND AN ALKYD RESIN PLANT.

2. MONICA INTEGRATED GLOBAL INDUSTRIES LIMITED PALM KERNEL CRUSHING /PROCESSING PLANT.

Peaks Pro is today a company and a major player in the Manufacturing sub – sector of the Nigeria economy. In view of its expansion, the company in 2007 moved into its state of art and ultra – modern industry complex located at Ohokobe – Afaraukwu Ibeku along Old Aba Road Umuahia, while at the same time retaining and maintaining its old factory at Amuzukwu – Ibeku also in Umuahia.
